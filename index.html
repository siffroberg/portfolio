ABOUT ME

SIF FROBERG

I am positive and outgoing, - I am committed and persistent when a task needs to be carried out.

I find both joy in working alone or together as a team.
I find it important to be able to do both.
strengthening and learning from each other is important
- I'm not done until I'm satisfied with the result.
I will say that my attention to detail takes me far.
However, it is also very important for me to have an overview, and I therefore have a very structured work approach.
I see myself as a proactive, committed and solution-oriented web designer
who thinks outside the box and that digital design is an exciting way to use my creative energy.

Through my education as a multimedia designer,
I get to know all the different parts of a design process and its development. So far I have worked with:

- Code with HTML5 and CSS3
- Design and prototyping in Adobe XD
- Graphic design and image optimization in Photoshop, Illustrator and LightRoom
- Video editing in Premiere Pro
